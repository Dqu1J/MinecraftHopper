Minecraft Crash Help

Verify Your Mojang Account Owns Minecraft

This will allow you to make sure that the purchase was successful and that issues you are experiencing are with the game, not with your account.

  1. Go to account.mojang.com/login and log in. If you're still using your username instead of your email address to sign in, it's recommended that you migrate your account.
  2. After logging in, you should see a list of games.
  3. If "Minecraft" is on this list, then you have purchased the game using this account, if you do not see Minecraft, then you have not purchased Minecraft using this account. Try logging in with other email addresses, it's possible that you accidentally created more than one Mojang account.
